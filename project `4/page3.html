HIS RACING CAREER

In addition to competing in three Italian Formula 4 rounds, Bearman made his debut in the ADAC Formula 4 Championship in 2020 with US Racing. During his participation in the Italian Championship, the British driver achieved two podium finishes. Bearman moved to Van Amersfoort Racing for the 2021 season in order to serve as a double agent in the Italian and German F4 Championships. Bearman became the first driver to win two consecutive Formula 4 titles in a single year. In September 2021, Bearman received a nomination for the Autosport BRDC Award in recognition of winning two Formula Four titles. Bearman also received the Henry Surtees Award for the most exceptional performance by a BRDC rising star in December 2021. In addition to his work in Formula 4, Bearman competed for Fortec Motorsports in the BRDC British Formula 3 Championship. Bearman's participation in the FIA Formula 3 Championship post-season test with Prema Racing was revealed on October 31, 2021. Bearman's announcement as a Prema Racing driver for the 2023 Formula 2 Championship was made on November 14, 2022. Bearman would finish the 2024 season with Prema Racing. He had to substitute for Carlos Sainz in the Saudi Arabian Grand Prix and finished in P7 being the youngest f1 drver to score points on debut. (Wikipedia, 2024)

ABOUT HIM

Oliver James Bearman (born 8 May 2005) is a British racing driver who is currently competing in the 2024 FIA Formula 2 Championship for Prema Racing and is a member of the Ferrari Driver Academy. He is a reserve driver for both Scuderia Ferrari and Haas F1 Team in Formula One. He made his Formula One debut with Scuderia Ferrari at the 2024 Saudi Arabian Grand Prix, substituting for Carlos Sainz Jr. (Wikipedia, 2024)
